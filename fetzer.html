A new site for some agricultural rebels

Fetzer wines was on the verge of a total rebrand as the rebels of wine agriculture and they needed an updated site to tell their story. Since day one, they were doing things differently, and they needed a beautiful website that showed their history, family and of course their wines. Fetzer always practiced organic farming and promoted biodiversity on their Northern California vineyards, making them "rebels" amungst winemakers. We wanted to showcase the organic famrers at their beautiful properties to make this site stand out.
